# Product Guidelines

## 1. Communication Style

*   **Tone:** All user-facing text, from UI labels to documentation, must be **Professional & Concise**. The language should be clear, direct, and unambiguous to support the fast-paced hospital environment. Avoid jargon where possible and prioritize efficiency in communication.

## 2. Branding and Visual Design

*   **Look and Feel:** The application's design will be **Friendly & Approchable**. It should feel welcoming and easy to navigate. This will be achieved through a color palette of soft, calming colors, rounded corners on UI elements, and clean, readable typography. The goal is to create a user experience that reduces stress, not adds to it.

## 3. User Experience (UX) Principles

The user experience will be guided by the following core principles:

*   **Efficiency First:** The system is a tool for busy professionals. Workflows must be a streamlined to minimize clicks and automate repetitive tasks. The application should feel fast and responsive.
*   **Clarity & Readability:** All information, especially complex data like no-show probability scores, must be presented in a way that is immediately understandable. Use clear labels, intuitive visualizations, and logical layouts to ensure users can grasp the information at a glance.
