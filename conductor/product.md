# Product Guide: Hospital No-Show Prediction System

## 1. Vision

To create a smart scheduling system for hospitals—driven by a network of autonomous ADK agents—that proactively reduces revenue loss and inefficiencies caused by patient no-shows. By leveraging machine learning, the system will identify high-risk appointments and enable staff to fill those slots with backup patients, ensuring a consistently full schedule.

## 2. Target Audience

The primary users of this system are **Hospital Staff**, including receptionists, schedulers, and administrative personnel responsible for managing patient appointments. The interface and workflows will be designed to integrate seamlessly into their daily tasks.

## 3. Core Functionality

The initial version will focus on three critical features:

*   **No-Show Probability Analysis:** For every upcoming appointment, the system will display a "no-show probability score." This score is generated by an ML model trained on historical patient data, considering factors like appointment history, time of day, and other relevant variables.
*   **Backup Patient Management:** When an appointment is flagged as high-risk for a no-show, the system will suggest a suitable backup patient from a pre-vetted waitlist.
*   **Automated Backup Agent:** An automated agent will continuously monitor upcoming appointments, identify potential no-shows, and proactively look for suitable backup patients, presenting options to the scheduling staff for confirmation.

## 4. Key Goals & Success Metrics

The project's success will be measured against the following goals:

*   **Primary Goal:** **Reduce the patient no-show rate.**
    *   *Metric:* A measurable decrease in the percentage of missed appointments month-over-month.
*   **Secondary Goal:** **Optimize resource allocation.**
    *   *Metric:* An increase in the number of filled appointment slots that were previously at high risk of being no-shows.
